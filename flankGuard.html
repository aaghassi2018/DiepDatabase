Flank Guard

Advantages

  • multiple bullet streams
  • consistent speed

Disadvantages

  • relatively normal or weak bullets
  • no speed boosts from guns

Tactics

POINTS

  • great tank for collecting points
  • move in one direction and face another so your bullets sweep the areas you pass

ATTACKING

  • try to avois attacks until you are very well upgraded. In the meantime, focus on point collection and upgrades
  • if attacked, just shoot and retreat. Unless the opponent is weak, you will not stand a chance
