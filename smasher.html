Smasher

Advantages

  • really high health
  • really high body damage
  • consistent speed (which can be really high)
  • more concentrated upgrades (only a few options)

Disadvantages

  • No bullets or guns, at all
  • no speed boost from guns (because they don't exist)
  • speed is more easily affected by other player's guns, when they shoot at you

Tactics

RESOURCE COLLECTION

  • Go for the more valuable, larger resources such as pentagons
  • Wait for larger pentagons to be taken down on health by others, then deliver the final blow

ATTACKING

  • Smashers can beat anyone whose goal is to smash into opponents so long as they are at full health
  • Work within bullet storms to attack opponents being pushed back by it, while being able to retreat somewhere if necessary
  • Don't work in areas of dispersed resources, they will slow you down whenever you crash into them. Work out in the open
