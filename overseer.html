Overseer

Advantages

  • rapid reload rate
  • wide, unpredictable spray of bullets, which is good for exploiting dispersed resources, blocking sprays of bullets right in front of you, and overwhelming enemies right in front of you
  • quick movement in opposite direction of shooting

Disadvantages

  • quickly dispersed spray of bullets, which is not good for targeting distant or specific targets (you need to be right in front of what you are shooting at)
  • slow movement in direction of shooting

Tactics

TACTICS

  • Using the speed machine guns have shooting in the opposite direction, quickly move to the back or unguarded spot of an opponent. Then turn your gun towards the player and inflict as much damage as possible before/while running away. Reload health and repeat.
  • Although this method may not eliminate the opponent quickly or directly, it will distract it until someone else can eliminate it. Alternatively, if someone else is already about to destroy the player, this tactic can let you get the final shot and all the points.
  • Especially useful tactic in Team DM.

TEAMWORK

  • Especially in Team DM, machine guns are valuable members of bullet storms. Machine guns add unpredictability and wide range.
