Overseer

Advantages

  • triangles/minions which will move on their own to collect points or move by your command
  • yo
  • yo

Disadvantages

  • slow movement speed with no speed bonus from guns
  • no guns or bullets
  • triangles are generally slow (need to prioritize bullet speed)

Tactics

DESTOYERS

  • Avoid destroyers. With one shot a destroyer can destroy all of your triangles/minions
  • If you do encounter a destroyer, and can move fast enough to avoid its bullets, press shift to disperse your triangles/minions when the destroyer bullet goes to hit it so that they aren't decimated. Then continue to pursue the destroyer with the triangles/minions.

TRAPPERS

  • When dealing with trappers, look for a weak point in their barricades of nodes and try to exploit it. If none exists, try threatening with the triangles/minions in some areas for them to build up more and wait for one to form. It is not a quick process.

POINT COLLECTION

  • Simply turn off auto fire if it is on and let the triangles/minions collect points themselves. Click the shift key to spread the triangles/minions away from the mouse.

    • ATTACKING

  • Use your triangles/minions as a shield to defend yourself. Opponents will generally shoot at your triangles/minions, so keeping them away from you can distract them, but if the opponent shoots you in this case you will be defenseless.
