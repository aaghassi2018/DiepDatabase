What is the game all about?

"the ultimate smash hit game!"

diep.io is a multiplayer arena where players can compete in free-for-all, team, domination, and etc. Players can shoot enemies and resources to gain points to level up

players can upgrade their tank at levels 15, 30, and 45

different classes like damage, movement speed, reload speed, etc. can be upgraded at each level up

Controls

It's just normal WASD controls to move around... you can use the arrow keys if you really insist.

Space to shoot! You can press and hold, and most of the time you will be pressing and holding...

Some cool things to things to know!

  • if you press the "y" key, the class upgrade tree will show up on screen, similar to the one on our homepage... VERY helpful
  • "c" toggles auto-spin... not quite sure why you would want to ever use that feature
  • "e" toggles auto-fire, it's kind of helpful sometimes
