Sniper

Advantages

  • powerful bullets
  • improved range of view
  • gun speed bonus

Disadvantages

  • low reload rate
  • gun speed rate is inconsistent due to low reload

Tactics

POINT COLLECTION

  • sniper is not good at getting many small resources
  • go for large resources like pentagons

LONE RANGER

  • try to isolate from groups and sit in cover of resources catching people off guard and picking them off as you can see them but they can't see you (try to get them when they are at the edge of your vision)

TEAMWORK

  • alternatively, try to focus and work within bullet storms
  • snipers can pick people off while bullet storms distract them
