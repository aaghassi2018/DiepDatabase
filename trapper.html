Advantages

  • nodes (bullets of a trapper) last a long time in one place, forming barricades

Disadvantages

Tactics

POINT COLLECTION

  • Trapper is not good at getting large areas of resources
  • go staright to the middle and ofcus on pentagons

ATTACKING

  • attacking and defending are one and the same as a trapper, use your nodes to protect yourself and surround the opponent

TEAMWORK

  • trappers provide defensive structures for bullet storms which allow them to push further against opponents
  • trappers leave mines for those who push bullet storms back
