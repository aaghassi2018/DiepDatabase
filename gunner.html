Gunner

Advantages

  • extremly rapid reload
  • multiple bullets fired at once
  • relatively powerful
  • focused, wide stream of bullets (not spread out)

Disadvantages

  • small individual bullets
  • relatively weak individual bullets until fully upgraded
  • generally needs to be mostly upgraded to be effective

Tactics

KEEP COVER

  • until fully upgraded, stay out of the middle and to areas which have many smaller resources. gunners are one of the best classes to fully take advantage of many smaller resources, but will not do as well to take on pentagons and associated rivals until fully upgraded

TEAMWOWRK

  • gunners are very valuable to bullet storms, filling in gaps, focusing on enemies, and generally adding to the necessary "storm"

UPGRADE

  • work to fully upgrade vefore taking on rivals. upgraded fully , the gunner can take on just about any situation. until then, it is a relatively weak class; however, it is optimal for situations in which the middle is too dangerous to risk visiting to upgrade
