Twin Flank

Advantages

  • fast reload
  • concentrated bullet streams (two guns each)
  • multiple bullet streams
  • strong bullets

Disadvantages

  • no speed benefit from guns
  • although you have multiple bullet streams, they face opposite directions therefore rarely are able to both be used against opponenets at the same time

Tactics

TEAMWORK

  • Twin Flanks act very similarly to twins in bullet storms, concentrating it on one area

POINT COLLECTION

  • Twin Flanks are very good at point collections with their multiple bullet streams
  • just turn on autofire and move around

NOT HARRASSMENT

  • although Twin Flanks function similarly to Twins in most cases, they do not have the speed benefit for harrassment tactics
