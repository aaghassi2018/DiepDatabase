Sandbox

Details

Sandbox mode is a playground to experiment with tank upgrades. There is nobody but you on each map. Press K to upgrade, or eliminate the resources around you in the small space. There is no center with pentagons, but there are triangles to destroy you from the center. These are your only opponents, but they aren't likely to kill you. This is, obviously, the fastest mode to upgrade in. Just hold "K" and you will be fully upgraded.
